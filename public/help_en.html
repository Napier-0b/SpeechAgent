Using SpeechAgent for voice chat

Setting up NetDuetto

Download NetDuetto and install it.
Open Google Chrome, play some music on YouTube.
Navigate to Settings->System->Sound->App Volume and Device Preference, change Google Chrome sound output to NetDuetto.
CHECK: PC stopped playing music.
Right click on the volume control icon in the taskbar, open Sound. Then navigate to Recording tab, open NetDuetto Properties.
Turn on "listen to this device" in Listen tab. Choose the playback device which you want to hear SpeechAgent voice from.
CHECK: PC started playing music again.

Opening the web app

SpeechAgent

Setting up VRChat

Run VRChat, open in-game settings, navigate to System->Microphone, change device to Yamaha NetDuetto.
WARNING: From now on, any sounds played in Google Chrome will be broadcasted in VRChat.
CHECK: You spoke into your microphone, SpeechAgent repeated it, and the mic icon in VRChat flashed red.
Done!
